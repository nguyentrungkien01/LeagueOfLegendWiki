Summoner spells are abilities that players can use for their champions on the Fields of Justice.

They are chosen in Champion Select before the start of the game. Each player is allowed two summoner spells chosen from a list. Summoner spells can be used in game to aid a player's champion in various ways as each spell has a unique effect. Some summoner spells increase in effectiveness relative to your champion's level, but some provide the same effect at champion level 1 as they do at champion level 18. They do not have any cost other than their cooldown.

All summoner spells, at the exception of Flash, Teleport, Hexflash, and Mark / Dash can be used while silenced.

Available summoner spell

A summoner spell's availability is based on the player's summoner level. At summoner level one, a player begins with 2 summoner spells to choose from. Then as a summoner reaches new levels, they unlock new summoner spells, up to summoner level 9.

Reduce Cooldown

There are three ways to reduce the cooldown of summoner spells.

  • Cosmic Insight
  • Ionian Boots of Lucidity
  • Howling Abyss aura

The 15 second cooldown of Smite is not affected by summoner spell cooldown reduction, but the rate at which Smite charges are generated is affected.

Trivia

  • For the first 15 seconds of a game on Summoner's Rift all summoner spells are disabled.
  • In-game Summoner Spell icon tooltips always show the true cooldown, after rune and item bonuses. Cooldown display may take a few seconds to update after items have been acquired or sold. [may need confirmation for enemy summoner spells]
  • Spells are map-specific. For example, if you play ARAM on Summoner's Rift , the available spells are Summoner's Rift spells.
  • Legend of the Poro King (Howling Abyss), Odyssey: Extraction (Crash Site) and Nexus Siege (Summoner's Rift) use or did use game mode specific summoner spells.
  • A few summoner spells originated as alpha items , such as Teleport having roots in the items Ancient Pocket Watch and Scroll of Teleportation.
    • This may be a borrowed gameplay design choice from DotA where powerful gameplay effects similar to summoner spells were accessible to players through items.

EDIT: PATCH 5.23 : "Fixed a number of broken interactions between the Summoner Spell cooldown reduction effects from Boots of Lucidity, Enchantment: Distortion and the Insight mastery" I will check this out latter.
Now, the reductions from Lucidity and Enchantment 'Distorsion' DOES STACK multiplicatively. (Flash with a 3:12 CD -192s- is for real, 300*0.75*0.85=192s :D)
In short, Lucidity Reduction and mastery Insight stack ADDITIVELY while Distorsion Reduction stacks MULTIPLICATIVELY with both Lucidity and Insight Reductions.
I have just tested all of that. (Edit: These tests were done on patch 5.22. THINGS HAVE CHANGED)
First, note that the reductions from Lucidity and Enchantment 'Distorsion' do not stack. If you take both, Distorsion will be applied and the 10% from Lucidity will be ignored.
Results are strange though :
Lucidity Reduction and mastery Insight stack ADDITIVELY : e.g. Flash' CD goes down to 225s. (300 * 0.75).
Distorsion Reduction and mastery Insight stack MULTIPLICATIVELY : e.g Flash' CD goes down to 217s. (300 * 0.85 * 0.90).
